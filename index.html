Lasagne With Dough-Balls

Serves 3-5 People

Ingredients For Lasagne:

  • 300-500g Of Mince*
  • An Onion
  • Half A Red pepper
  • 6-8 Mushrooms
  • 1-2 Garlic Cloves
  • A Sprinkle Of Basil
  • 200-300g Of Grated Cheese**
  • A Jar Of White Sauce
  • A Jar Of Bolognase Sauce
  • 8-12 Sheets* Of Lasagne Pasta
      • * Depends on whether you do 2 or 3 layers
      **or how much you want

      Ingredients For Dough-Balls:

      WARNING THE DOUGH-BALLS NEED TO SIT AND RISE FOR 1.5-2 Hours!!

    • 300g Of Wholemeal / White Bread Flour
    • TBSP Of Caster Sugar
    • TSP Of Salt
    • 50g Of Butter
    • 175ml Of Water
    • Oil
    • A Large Mixing Bowl

      • What Equipment You Will Need

    • Frying Pan
    • An Oven Dish
    • Baking Tray
    • Baking Paper
    • A Saucepan
    • A Small Snack-Pot Or Bowl
    • Cling-Film

      • Method For Dough-Balls

    • 1st put the 175ml of water in the sacepan then add the 50g of butter heat until steaming then mix the butter in with the water and set asside to cool; meanwhile pour the flour in the mixing boal with caster sugar and salt
    • 2nd When the butter and water is cooled add it to the flour and knead then knock the air out of it and place a layer of cling film gently oil it place the oiled side of the cling film on the side facing the dough and leave to set
    • When the dough has risen place a baking tray and place gently oiled baking paper over the top of it then split the dough into the size of a walnut and place then 1/2 a centimeter apart then put in the oven for 20-30-mins

      • Method For The Lasagne:

    • 1st for the lasagne fry the onions, garlic, Mushroom and Pepper in wok or pan until onions are slightly browned. Then add the mince. Then add bolognase sauce.
    • 2nd Put 1/3 or 1/2 of the mince in an oven dish add a layer of pasta, the pasta has to cover the whole of the dish then add 1/3 or 1/2 of the white sauce
    • 3rd add some mince on top of the white sauce if you chose to do 2 layers stop here and go to 5th paragraph if you chose 3 carry on to the next paragraph
    • 4th add mince on top of the white sauce then pasta which covers the whole top of the baking dish add the rest of the white sauce.
    • 5th on top of the white sauce add the cheese and leve in the oven for 25-40 mins keep an eye on it!!
